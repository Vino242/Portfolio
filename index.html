About

I'm Vincent Noel Mark, 28, a graduate communication designer specializing in UI Design.

I create clear, functional interfaces with a focus on structure, typography and visual precision. My approach is conceptual and reduced — understand, analyze, design.

Beyond digital design, I work artistically, illustratively and photographically. I'm drawn to image composition, light and the tension between stillness and expression.
